Who We Are

Pioneering Sustainable Construction in Somalia

Founded in 2009, ECO-CORE DEVELOPERS was established with one mission: deliver world-class construction and engineering services tailored to Somalia's unique environment, culture, and development needs.

Over 15 years, we have grown from a small civil works contractor into one of Somalia's most trusted multidisciplinary construction groups — operating across residential, commercial, infrastructure, and advisory sectors with $240M+ in delivered project value.
